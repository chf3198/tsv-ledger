How to Get Your Amazon Data
  1. Go to Amazon.com → Your Account
  2. Navigate to Privacy & Data Protection
  3. Click "Request my data"
  4. Select "Your Orders" and "Subscriptions"
  5. Amazon will email ZIP files within 30 days
