## 🚀 **How to Use the Employee Benefits Filter**

### Step 1: Navigate to Employee Benefits
1. Open the TSV Ledger at `http://localhost:3000`
2. Click **"👥 Employee Benefits"** in the sidebar menu
3. You'll see the Amazon Item Filter section

### Step 2: Select Items for Employee Benefits
1. Click on the **"Select Items for Employee Benefits"** dropdown
2. Items are organized by category (Employee Amenities, Office Supplies, etc.)
3. Type to search for specific items (e.g., "coffee", "paper towels")
4. Select items that should be categorized as employee benefits
5. Selected items show with prices and categories

### Step 3: Apply the Filter
1. Click **"Apply Filter"** to process your selections
2. View the **Employee Benefits Summary**:
   - Total benefits value
   - Number of items and orders
   - Monthly breakdown
   - Category distribution
3. See the **Filtered Items List** with details

### Step 4: Analyze Results
- **Monthly Breakdown**: See spending by month
- **Category Analysis**: Understand benefit types
- **Business Impact**: View annual projections and tax implications
- **Export**: Save results for accounting/reporting
