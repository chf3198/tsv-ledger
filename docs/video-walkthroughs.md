# Video Walkthroughs

## Overview

Video walkthroughs provide visual, narrated guidance for TSV Ledger features, combining screen recordings with expert narration, on-screen annotations, and interactive elements. These videos are designed for different learning styles and skill levels, ensuring comprehensive coverage of all application capabilities.

## Video Production Standards

### Technical Specifications
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (Full HD) for crisp visuals
- **Frame Rate**: 30 FPS for smooth motion
- **Audio**: Stereo 44.1kHz with noise reduction
- **Format**: MP4 with H.264 video codec
- **Duration**: 3-15 minutes per video for optimal engagement
- **File Size**: Optimized for web streaming (under 100MB per video)

### Content Standards
- **Clear Narration**: Professional voice-over with proper pacing
- **Visual Clarity**: High-contrast text and clear demonstrations
- **Pacing**: 1-2 seconds pause after key actions for comprehension
- **Annotations**: On-screen highlights, arrows, and callouts
- **Accessibility**: Closed captions and transcripts for all videos
