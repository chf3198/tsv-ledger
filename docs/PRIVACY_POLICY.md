# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date**: February 2025

---

## 1. Overview

TSV Ledger is designed with privacy as a core principle.
We collect minimal data and process most information locally.

---

## 2. Data We DO NOT Collect

- Your expense data (processed locally in browser)
- Your bank statements or financial records
- Amazon order history details
- Allocation decisions you make
- Export files you generate

---

## 3. Data We MAY Collect (Cloud Sync Users Only)

If you enable cloud sync features:

- Email address (for authentication)
- Encrypted expense metadata (for sync functionality)
- Usage timestamps (for sync coordination)

---

## 4. Data We Collect (All Users)

- Anonymous usage analytics (page views, feature usage)
- Error logs (to improve service reliability)

---

## 5. Local-First Architecture

Your financial data stays on your device by default:

1. **CSV files** are parsed in your browser
2. **Expense data** is stored in browser localStorage
3. **Exports** are generated client-side
4. **No server upload** unless you enable cloud sync

---

## 6. Third-Party Services

| Service    | Purpose                   | Data Shared            |
| ---------- | ------------------------- | ---------------------- |
| Cloudflare | Hosting & CDN             | IP address, page views |
| GitHub     | Authentication (optional) | Email, username        |

---

## 7. Data Retention

- **Local data**: Until you clear browser storage
- **Cloud sync data**: Until you delete your account
- **Analytics**: 90 days, then aggregated/anonymized

---

## 8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** your data (export feature available)
- **Delete** your data (clear localStorage or delete account)
- **Opt-out** of cloud sync (use local-only mode)
- **Request** information about data we hold

---

## 9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under 18.
We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

---

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We will update the "Effective Date" when changes are made.
Continued use constitutes acceptance of changes.

---

## 11. Contact

For privacy inquiries, contact the repository maintainers.

---

## 12. Compliance

This policy is designed to comply with:

- CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
- GDPR principles (for international users)
- General privacy best practices

---

_TSV Ledger: Your data, your device, your control._
