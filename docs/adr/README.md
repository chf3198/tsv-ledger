## Template

```markdown
# ADR-NNN: Title

**Date**: YYYY-MM-DD
**Status**: Proposed | Accepted | Deprecated | Superseded

## Context

What is the issue? What forces are at play?

## Decision

What is the change proposed?

## Consequences

What becomes easier? What becomes harder?
```
